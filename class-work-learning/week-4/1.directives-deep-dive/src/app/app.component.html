Directives are angular attributes/properties initialized to TS variables, expressions or methods.When initialized (most of them) they are used inside [] for data binding to the TS variables, expressions or methods to be active

If the ts variable/method that a directive is initialized to is true then the element on which the directive is placed will be displayed else will not be displayed(*ngIf).Structural directives are used for conditional rendering in angular( especially use of *ngIf).
